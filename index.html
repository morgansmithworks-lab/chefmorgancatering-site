FAQ

A few common questions. These help search engines (and people) understand our services.

What areas do you serve?
We serve the Big Island of Hawaiʻi and can travel for destination events by arrangement.
Do you accommodate dietary needs?
Yes — vegetarian, vegan, and gluten‑free menus are available with advance notice.
How far in advance should we book?
For peak seasons and weddings, 2–3 months is ideal. For private dinners, 2–4 weeks usually works.
Do you provide rentals and staff?
We can coordinate rentals and service staff as needed for your event.
